Call for Paper

The Editor welcomes submissions of research papers and book reviews, for the 31st August 2024 edition of the Journal.
The journal is interested in well-researched and original papers on all aspects of English Language and Literary Studies.
The Journal strives for robust academic approach to research and scholarship with the aim of creating a platform for academic scholarship and research on Nigeria, Africa and the world with researches in English Language and literature as the foundation for such intellectual inquiries....

How to Submit

Authors must submit manuscripts of not less than 3,500 words and not more 5000 words, with an abstract of about 250 words.
Electronic Submissions should be sent as email attachments using a standard word-processing program to:

e.irany@tsuniversity.edu.ng

References MUST be presented in a consistent style sheet; the Editors prefer APA 7th Edition Or MLA 8th Edition. Interested contributors should submit their papers on or before 15th of August 2024.
Your file should be in this format: Surname (capital Letters), and other Names, institutional affiliation, email address, phone numbers, Brief Bio. ....
